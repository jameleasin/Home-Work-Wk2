About Me

My name is Jamie. I've dabbled in code for most of my life, completing a Certificate IV in Multimedia Design and I.T with a programming stream in 2013. Since then I have persude my love of animal health completing my Certificate IV in veterinary Nursing in 2017. I have moved from GP work to ECC work and have enjoyed learning in a more critical role.
I have decided to move back into a more creative space for work and have fallen back into programming with ease. I am very excited to learn all the new languages that have come about since 2013 and to really take my programming skills to the next level. I enjoy the front end design aspect of web development, making things asthetically pleasing and creating more interactive web applications.
